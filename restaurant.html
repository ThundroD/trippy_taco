The Trippy Taco

Our Trippy Story

One day, long ago, the founder of Trippy Taco was lost in the desert. He only had 3 tortillas but nothing to put in them, until he found a catcus! Not knowing what this plant was he decided to put in in between his tortillas and have his lunch. A few moments later, he realized something was different...he realized he had eaten Peyote!!! The entire day the desert transformed colors, things did not look the same. He followed bright lights until he found a small town and found safety once again. That day he decided he was going to open Trippy Taco!

Our Food

Our food is made of the freshest, all natural, organic ingredients. Only something mother nature would eat herself...ALSO...all dishes include peyote. You can ask your waiter for a mild trip, spicy trip, or if your wild like our founder, a super spicy trip! We serve breakfast, lunch and dinner. Also don't miss our tasty deserts
